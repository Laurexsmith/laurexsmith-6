    Welfarism

    welfarism is a theory that well-being, what is good for someone or what makes a life worth living, is the only thing that has intrinsic value.

    Empowerment

    Empowerment is the degree of autonomy and self-determination in people and in communities. This enables them to represent their interests in a responsible and self-determined way, acting on their own authority.

    Ethics and sustainability

    meeting our own needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. In addition to natural resources, we also need social and economic resources. Sustainability is not just environmental- ism.
